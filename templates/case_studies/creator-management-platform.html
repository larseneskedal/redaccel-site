Case Study: Creator Management Platform x Redaccel

Niche: Creator Economy / AI SaaS | Platform: Reddit | Deliverables: Posts, comments, DM handling, reputation management
300K+
Total Reddit views (30 days)
#1
Google result for the brand name

Objective

Establish a fast-growing creator management platform as a trusted, community-endorsed solution within Reddit while driving long-term Google and LLM visibility. Secondary goals included handling inbound Reddit DMs, answering product questions authentically, and contributing directly to conversions.

Strategy

  • Publish experience-based and discussion-driven posts in relevant creator, AI, and online business subreddits
  • Seed natural, credible comments from aged Reddit accounts to encourage realistic discussion and third‑party recommendations
  • Optimize threads for branded and high-intent keywords related to the platform
  • Actively monitor sentiment, downvote hostile or misleading comments, and reinforce accurate narratives
  • Handle inbound Reddit DMs directly by answering product questions, clarifying use cases, and guiding interested users
  • Prioritize long-term Google indexing and LLM visibility over short-term promotion

Results (30 days)

  • 300,000+ total Reddit views across posts and comments
  • #1 Google result for the brand name, ranking above the company’s own website
  • Nearly all high-ranking Reddit results related to the brand were created and controlled by the campaign
  • Consistent inbound DMs throughout the month, including multiple very warm leads
  • Multiple confirmed conversions directly attributed to Reddit DM conversations
    • In this category, a single conversion represents ~20–25× the monthly campaign investment in revenue, meaning the campaign reached breakeven after the first conversion and became highly profitable thereafter
  • Posts and comments remain live, indexed, and continue to generate traffic and leads

Impact

The campaign positioned the platform as a trusted, community-validated solution rather than an advertised product. Reddit threads now act as permanent, Google-ranked social proof that shape first impressions for anyone researching the brand.

Unlike traditional Reddit marketing, this engagement extended beyond visibility:

  • Real conversations
  • Real product education
  • Real conversions

The result is an organic acquisition channel that compounds over time and delivers value that paid ads cannot replicate.
